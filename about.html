Who Am I?

I am a developer and UX designer. I learned to program while I was attending New York City Dev Bootcamp. It was a life changing experience and I have since learned full stack development. I believe in simplicity,innovation, and creativity when designing and building web applications. When I am not cranking out elegant code or designing beautiful interfaces, you will find me riding my bike on the west side highway or drinking coffee at a coffee shop.
